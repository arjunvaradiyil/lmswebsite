FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What subjects do you offer?

We offer all subjects from KG to PG level including Mathematics, Science, English, Hindi, Malayalam, and many more languages and subjects.

Do you provide online classes?

Yes, we provide both offline and online classes. You can choose home tuition or live online sessions based on your preference.

How do you track student progress?

We provide monthly assessments, regular tests, and detailed progress reports. Parents also get real-time updates through our WhatsApp support group.

What languages do you teach in?

We offer classes in English, Hindi, Arabic, Malayalam, Tamil, Kannada, Marathi, Odiya, and many other languages.

Do you offer group classes?

Yes, we offer both one-to-one and group classes. You can choose based on your learning preference and requirements.

How do I get started?

Simply fill out our registration form above or contact us at +91 7907 041 628. We'll arrange a consultation and start your learning journey.
